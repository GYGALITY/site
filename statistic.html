What position does LeBron James play?
LeBron primarily plays as a small forward, but his incredible versatility allows him to also play as a point guard or power forward when needed.

How many NBA titles has LeBron James won?
LeBron James has won 4 NBA championships: two with the Miami Heat (2012, 2013), one with the Cleveland Cavaliers (2016), and one with the Los Angeles Lakers (2020).
