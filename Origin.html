Origin Story

BABY LEBRON

LeBron James was born on December 30, 1984, in Akron, Ohio. He was raised by his mother, Gloria James, who was just 16 years old when he was born. Life was difficult for them — they moved frequently and faced financial hardships, sometimes even struggling to find stable housing. Despite these challenges, Gloria did everything she could to support her son. From a young age, LeBron showed remarkable talent in sports, especially basketball, and it quickly became clear he had a special gift.

"Don’t be afraid to dream big. It all starts with a ball and a dream."

TEEN LEBRON

As a teenager, LeBron attended St. Vincent–St. Mary High School in Akron, where he became a national basketball sensation. His skills on the court attracted major media attention, and he was featured on the cover of Sports Illustrated while still in high school — a rare feat. He led his team to multiple state championships and was widely regarded as the best high school basketball player in the country. During this time, he also dealt with the pressure of fame and the expectations of becoming a future NBA star.

"Don’t be afraid of failure. This is the way to succeed."

ADULT LEBRON

LeBron James was drafted into the NBA in 2003 as the first overall pick by the Cleveland Cavaliers. Since then, he has had a legendary career, winning multiple NBA championships with the Miami Heat, Cleveland Cavaliers, and Los Angeles Lakers. Off the court, LeBron is known for his philanthropy, including opening the “I PROMISE” School in Akron for at-risk children. He has also become a successful businessman and activist, using his platform to speak out on social and political issues. Today, he is considered one of the greatest basketball players of all time.

"I think team first. It allows me to succeed, it allows my team to succeed."

About This Page

This page provides an in-depth look at the life and career of LeBron James, one of the greatest basketball players of all time. From his humble beginnings growing up in Akron, Ohio, facing economic hardships and personal challenges, to rising as a global sports icon, LeBron's journey is a powerful story of perseverance, talent, and dedication. The page highlights key moments in his life, his early passion for basketball, and how he overcame obstacles to achieve success on and off the court, inspiring millions around the world.
